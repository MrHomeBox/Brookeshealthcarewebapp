Mental Health Needs

What are Mental Health Needs?

Mental health conditions or disorders are medical conditions that affect a person's thinking, mood, behavior, or overall mental functioning. Examples include depression, anxiety disorders, schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, and eating disorders.

We offer a wide range of additional services

Services offered to individuals with mental health needs include counseling and therapy, medication management, hospitalization and inpatient treatment, support groups, case management, crisis helplines, rehabilitation programs, and online/digital mental health resources. These services aim to provide emotional support, treatment, crisis intervention, skills development, and community integration to improve mental well-being and quality of life.

Treating mental health needs involves assessment, therapy, medication (if necessary), supportive services, ongoing monitoring, self-care, and long-term management. Working with mental health professionals is important for personalized support.
