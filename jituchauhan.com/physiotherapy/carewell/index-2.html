About Brookes Healthcare center

Brookeshealthcare is a service that offers support for individuals with various mental health needs, challenging behaviors, learning disabilities, complex needs, and substance and alcohol misuse. They provide inclusive group settings for individuals with a forensic past, those on CTO (Community Treatment Order), and both males and females aged 18 and above. The service offers 24-hour support, supported accommodation, and respite services to assist individuals in their journey toward better mental health and overall well-being.
